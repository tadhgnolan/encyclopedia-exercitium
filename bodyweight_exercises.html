Press-Up Take a prone position on the floor. Keep body in a straight line from head to hips to feet. Place hands flat on the floor at chest height, facing forward. Upper arms should be at a 45° to body. In a steady controlled manner, press body away from floor until arms are almost locked out. Return body to floor in the same controlled manner.

Bodyweight Lunge Start in a neutral position, arms by sides, standing straight, shoulders back, head stacked over pelvis and pelvis stacked over ankles. Fold arms in front of chest and step forward in a controlled manner, back straight, lowering until front leg is bent at just over a 90° angle. Keep knee in line with foot and don't allow it to bend past the toes. Return to the starting position in a controlled manner.

Bodyweight Squat Start in a neutral position, arms by sides, standing straight, shoulders back, head stacked over pelvis and pelvis stacked over ankles, feet shoulder width apart facing slightly out. Fold arms in front of chest and lower body in a controlled manner as if sitting back until knees are at an angle of slightly less than 90°. Keep knees in line with feet throughout & don't allow them to go past toes. Return to starting position in a controlled manner.

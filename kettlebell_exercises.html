Kettlebell Press Standing with feet roughly shoulder width apart, squat down and grab the kettlebell. Keeping your back straight, return to a standing position. Lift kettlebell to shoulder position, tucking elbow into side as you do. From this starting position, extend your arm, pressing the kettlebell overhead, then return to the starting position. Make sure to keep correct posture throughout, tightening your core and keeping your back straight.

Kettlebell Lunge Standing with feet roughly hip distance apart, squat down and grab the kettlebell. Keeping your back straight and core tight, return to a standing position. Lift kettlebell with both handand hold in front of chest, this is the starting position. Step forward with one leg and lower into a near 90 degree lunge position, making sure to keep a neutrally aligned posture with back straight, core tight and that your knee tracks following the lead foot and does not pass your toes. Return to starting position.

Kettlebell Squat Standing with feet roughly shoulder width apart, toes pointing slightly outward squat down and grab the kettlebell. Keeping your back straight and core tight, return to a standing position. Lift kettlebell with both handand hold in front of chest, this is the starting position. Squat down to just above a 90 degree angle, making sure to keep a neutrally aligned posture with back straight, core tight and that your knees track following your feet and do not pass your toes. Return to starting position.
